ABOUT
BE
CLUB

BE Club is the scientific student club of the École Nationale Supérieure de Biotechnologie (ENSBiotech), Algeria. We bring together students who are passionate about Biotechnology and Information Technology — two fields that together define the science of tomorrow.

Who We Are

MISSION & VALUES

🎯

OUR MISSION

To build a community where science and technology intersect. BE Club empowers students to develop real skills — in the lab and behind the keyboard — while fostering collaboration, curiosity, and innovation across disciplines.

🔭

OUR VISION

We believe the scientists and engineers of the future will be fluent in both biology and code. BE Club exists to give students that head start — through workshops, events, research projects, and a strong peer community.

🤝

COMMUNITY

More than a club — a network. We connect students with professionals, academics, and industry leaders, creating opportunities that go beyond the classroom walls.

⚗️

HANDS-ON LEARNING

Theory is the starting point. We run real lab sessions, coding sprints, and science events where members gain practical experience that complements their academic journey.
